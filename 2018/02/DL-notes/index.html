Deep Learning on Coursera

Posted on 2018-02-13 | In Study Notes | | Views:

Target

Learn Deep Learning provided by Andrew Ng on Coursera so here is some notes for me to remind.

  • Loss function: the error for a single training example

  • Cost function: the average of the loss functions of the entire training set.

  • derivative just means slope of the line

  • vector/matrix multiplication is faster than for-loop

  • High bias: too many wrong prediction; High variance: too overfitting training data
